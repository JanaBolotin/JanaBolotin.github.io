Jana Bolotin is a filmmaker and media artist. Her works include animation, autobiography and commercial documentary. Jana’s autobiographical film, Alo! 86th Street has been officially selected by the Olympia Film Festival, the Tacoma Film Festival and Seattle’s Local Sightings Film Festival. Professionally, Jana has worked as an associate producer in nonfiction television for A&E and the Biography Channel. She is also the co-producer of The United States of Football, a feature length documentary about the long-term neurological effects of playing football. Jana holds an MFA in Digital Arts + New Media from UC Santa Cruz.
